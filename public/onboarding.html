Graderbot

Let's get you set up with GraderBot

Graderbot is an exemplar application for the LLM Sandbox. It is designed to give you a first pass at potential grades for your student's short-essay style answers to your questions.

Important Disclaimer

Graderbot uses Generative AI to help you grade your student's responses. Its responses are not guaranteed to be accurate. You should compare your own assessment of each student's work with GraderBot.

Let's get started

To get started, we need some information about you, which department you are with, and the course that you are grading. This all helps provide context to GraderBot to help produce more accurate results.
